About Us

In Safe Hands is an independent Irish healthcare company based in Mullingar, County Westmeath, Ireland. Our mission is to enable all our customers to protect the health and wellbeing of themselves and their loved ones by providing them with easy access to high quality, effective PPE equipment to help keep them safe during the Covid-19 pandemic. We strive to offer our customers the best protection we can one product at a time.
